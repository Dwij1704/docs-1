Use AI Apps to create Custom Plugins in Amazon Q Business
Equip xpander connectors and tools as Custom Plugins in Amazon Q Business.

In this quick tutorial, you'll learn how to use xpander connectors and tools as Custom Plugins in Amazon Q business. This allows you to extend your Amazon Q Business applications to connect to APIs and take actions.

1. Log in to the xpander platform, and go to the Connectors section.
2. Enable any connector you wish to use by providing authentication details.

> ðŸ“˜ If you do not wish to enter your credentials to enable a connector, you can complete this tutorial by using a built-in tool (which does not require your credentials). In that case, do not enable any connectors.

3. In the xpander console, go to the AI Apps section and click New AI App
4. In the right panel, click "Connect" next to Amazon Q Business.
5. In the integration wizard, enter the details of the account where your Amazon Q Business application is running, and click `Start Integration`
6. Provide a name for your AI App under `Integration name`, and select your Amazon Q Business application from the `Application` dropdown menu.
7. If you enabled any connectors in your xpander account, select them now, and in the next step, select the Operations (API calls) you would like to have enabled as the GPT actions. 

> ðŸš§ Amazon Q Business applications only support 3 simultaneous Custom Plugins, with a maximum of 8 operations per Custom Plugin. Make sure to not cross this limit.

> ðŸ“˜ If you haven't enabled any connectors, simply skip this step and select xpander tools.

8. In the Tools step, select the tools you would like to have enabled as Custom Plugins in your Amazon Q Business application.
9. When you click Done, xpander will push your Connector and Tool configuration to your Amazon Q Business application as Custom plugins.

Congratulations - you equipped your Amazon Q Business application with Custom Plugins, based on xpander connectors and/or tools.
