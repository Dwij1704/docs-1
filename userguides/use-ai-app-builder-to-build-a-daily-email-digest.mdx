#Use AI App builder to build a daily email digest
Get a daily digest with your favorite topics

Follow these steps to use the AI App builder to create a daily email digest that collects data by using xpander tools, and sends the collected information (news, sports, stocks...) to your email address.

1. Login to the xpander platform
2. Go to the AI App builder
3. Click the connectors button and skip adding any Connector. In the Tools step, select the Email tool as well as tools such as Stock market, News and Sports.

> ðŸ“˜ [Click here](https://docs.xpander.ai/docs/early-access-program-getting-started#terminology) for a reminder of what xpander Connectors and Tools are

4. In the chat window, enter the following prompt (feel free to adjust according to your interests)

> Write a digest by using all the tools at your disposal. I'm interested in Basketball, tech news and stock, and Generative AI advancements

5. After you see the response, enter the following prompt

> Email the result to me at [your@email.com](mailto:your@email.com)

6. Check that you received the email with the digest (check the spam folder as well), and you like the structure and content. Otherwise, go back to the chat and ask for modifications
7. Click the "Save as AI App" button
8. Fill in the App name. You can see the instructions that the Agentic automation will execute
9. Set up a schedule for the Agentic automation, and click Save App. 

Enjoy your daily digest!
